A little bit about Krypton

Krypton is a symmetrical
encryption open-source software
created by NotAidan.

Krypton was a project
created in the span
of two months.


What can Krypton do?

Krypton can encrypt ascii characters.
Krypton cannot encrypt
anything besides US-ASCII encoded characters
